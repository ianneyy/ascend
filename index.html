About Level Up International
Manpower Services Corporation

Level Up specializes in recruiting, training, and deploying highly skilled Filipino workers to international clients worldwide. We provide comprehensive workforce solutions covering recruitment, talent assessment, pre-departure training, outsourcing, and career development.

Founded with a mission to uphold the dignity of labor and create meaningful opportunities for Filipino workers, we operate under POEA regulations and international labor standards.

🎯 Vision

To be the leading provider of skilled manpower solutions connecting Filipino talent with global market partners.

🚀 Mission

To recruit, train, and deploy highly skilled Filipino workers efficiently and ethically with world-class standards.

🌱 Goals

To create long-term partnerships and growth opportunities for both workers and international employers.
